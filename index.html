Let's Play Squares

Read The Basic of Squares if you have never played before. The cost per square is $3 and that square will be in play for all three of the thanksgiving games. We will treat each of the three NFL games as a new square game. So you are essentially buying a square for $1 for each NFL game.

Everyone will pick the square(s) that they want and once its filled we can assign scores at random to each of the three games. Each game will have different values of the squares. In the event that we have a game cancelled or postponed, we will split that money into the remaining game(s).

If we get the board to fill up really fast, I will open up a second game. If the first or second board doesn't get filled 100% those squares will remain empty. If those empty squares win, the money for that win will roll forward into the next quarter. If that square wins the final quarter of that game, it will roll into the next game's 1st quarter. If it is the final game of the day and an empty square wins, the money shall be awarded to the last person who won a square. If there is any other scenario I haven't covered, I reserve the right to make a final decision.

The payouts will happen at the end of the 1st Quarter, Half Time, 3rd Quarter, End of Game. Each are worth $25 per NFL game. Notice that it is not the end of the 4th quarter, but the end of the game. So if we go into overtime, that will be what pays out.

After you make your selections, you must pay me immediately or your squares will be deleted. Please include the name you used on the square board in the payment memo. You can send payment via Paypal (adamdiel@gmail.com), Google Pay (adamdiel@gmail.com), or Venmo (@Adam-Diel). If you have any questions, need help with the board, or need me to change/delete a square email me at adamdiel@gmail.com or text me. Good Luck!
