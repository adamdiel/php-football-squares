Let's Play Squares

Read The Basic of Squares if you have never played before. The cost per square is $3 and that square will be in play for all three of the thanksgiving games. Everyone will pick the square(s) that they want and once its filled we can assign scores at random to each of the three games. Each game will have different values of the squares. In the event that we have a game cancelled or postponed, we will split that money into the remaining game(s).
